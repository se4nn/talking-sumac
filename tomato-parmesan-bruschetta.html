Fresh Tomato & Parmesan Bruschetta

Starters Quick

A perfect starter for any meal, but a particular favourite if youâ€™re cooking up something Italian for dinner.

Ingredients

  • The Bread
  • 1 Loaf of Ciabatta
  • 2 Tbsp of Olive Oil
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Sea Salt (to taste)
  • The Topping
  • 6 Ripe Tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp of Balsamic Vinegar
  • Bunch of Fresh Basil

Method

This recipe should take around 5 minutes to complete

  1. Crush the garlic cloves then add them to a large griddle pan on a medium heat with the olive oil.
  2. Slice up the ciabatta into 1 inch slices and add them to the griddle pan, cooking them until lightly brown.
  3. Grab the tomatoes and cut into thin slices then lightly season with salt.
  4. Use a cheese slicer (or vegetable peeler) to shave the parmesan onto the bread, then add the tomato.
  5. Grab the basil, remove the leaves and pile on top. I love fresh basil so I'm always generous when dishing it out.
  6. Add a small splash of balsamic vinegar and serve.
