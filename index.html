Roasted Red Pepper & Red Lentil Soup

Vegan Protein Quick

You can throw this dish together in around 25 minutes meaning you’ll have more time to spend doing whatever else it is that you enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 120g of Dried Red Lentils
  • 1 Vegetable Stock Cube
  • 2 Sweet Red Peppers
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • 1 Teaspoon of Paprika
  • 1 Teaspoon of Sumac
  • To serve
  • Soy Yoghurt
  • Toasted Pumpkin Seeds
  • Chopped Parsley

Method

This recipe should take around 25 minutes to complete

  1. Set your oven to 220oC and place the sweet red pepper directly onto a rack, leave them to cook turning once for around 20 minutes.
  2. Whilst they’re in the oven, cook the lentils as per the instructions on the pack adding the vegetable stock into the cooking water.
  3. Drain the lentils and place them in a food processor with the garlic clove(s), paprika and sumac.
  4. When the peppers are done (they should be slightly black on the outside) take the green stalks out and add them straight into the food processor (seeds and all).
  5. Turn the food processor on and keep it going until you reach the consistency you prefer. I like mine to be smooth, so I’ll keep it going for ages and ages.
  6. To serve, pour into a bowl and spoon some of the soy yoghurt on top, then garnish with some toasted pumpkin seeds.
