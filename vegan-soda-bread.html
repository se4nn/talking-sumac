Vegan Soda Bread

Vegan Bread

Like seemingly a lot of the population, I have taken up baking to kill some time through lockdown. Over the weekend we'd started to run out of bread, and as we were lacking yeast I put my fathers Irish roots to use and put together this simple vegan soda bread for you all to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 450g of Plain/Wholemeal Flour
  • 150ml of Oatly (or Equivalent)
  • 300g of Soy Yoghurt
  • 1 Teaspoon of Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt

Method

This recipe should take around 25 minutes to complete

  1. Set your oven to 230oC.
  2. Add the dry ingredients to a mixing bowl and make a well in the middle.
  3. Combine the Oatly and yoghurt together in a bowl and pour straight into the well.
  4. Mix either with your hands or a kneading attachment on a food mixer until a sticky but not wet dough is formed.
  5. Turn out the dough into a well floured surface and shape into a ball.
  6. Place the bowl in a round baking tray about 4cm deep and score a cross into the top.
  7. Place the dough into the oven and bake for 10 minutes.
  8. Reduce the oven to 200oC and bake for a further 30-40 minutes or until the loaf sounds hollow.
  9. When done turn out onto a wire cooling rack and enjoy.
